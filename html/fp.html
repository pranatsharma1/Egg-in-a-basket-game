EGG IN A BASKET

Rules and Instructions

  • Initially there will be an egg placed on a static basket.
  • There will be moving baskets above each other.
  • The player have to pop up the egg from the basket such that the egg lands in the above basket.
  • On each successful landing, the player will be awarded a score of 1.
  • There will be 5 lives available for the player.
  • Some baskets will be having thorns in them.So,if the egg lands in such a basket,one life will be lost
  • There will be some baskets with extra features like up and down movement etc.
  • For winning a game the player would have to reach a score of 30.
